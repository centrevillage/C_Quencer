Ver 1.0.0:
* huge performance improvement
* add INSTABILITY mode (press TAP -> press START -> press FUNC)
	* FILL: CLOCK LFO MOD (select LFO rate & amp)
	* ROT: SYNC MODE (0: mix(default), 1: loose sync)
	* SCALE: VIBRATO (0-15: delayed vibrato(selectable rate), 16-31: normal vibrato(selectable rate)
	* PAT: PITCH OVERSHOOT (0-15: overshoot above, 16-31: overshoot below)
* add new Waveform(Log Curve) to OSC2 (at 6 position)
